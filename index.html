About Me

Hey! I'm Sadman, a pixel and low-poly 3D artist specializing in creating Minecraft assets. I'm familiar with various styles and always eager to work on something new and unique.

Currently, I've been contributing to several Minecraft server projects, such as AkumaMC and TavernMC. Before that, I was involved with notable teams and Minecraft Marketplace teams.

Aside from my passion for art, I'm also exploring Minecraft development while creating my own unique server, which will further help me develop my own distinctive art style!
